OUR PHILOSOPHY

This isn't new thinking. It's proven thinking -
applied to service ops.

The most powerful operational revolutions in history didn't happen by working harder. They
happened by redesigning the system itself.

Henry Ford took inspiration from meatpacking plants in Chicago, where conveyor belts moved work to stationary workers. He flipped this into the assembly line - cutting car production time from 12 hours to just 90 minutes by simplifying tasks and creating flow. When repetitive work caused high turnover, he doubled wages - not out of generosity, but to stabilize workers and boost output - turning efficiency into a self-reinforcing system of growth.

- The Ford Assembly Line Principle, 1913

Ford didn't just build cars faster. He redesigned the system around the work - so every step fed the next without friction, waste, or human error slowing things down.

That's exactly what Fixolutions does for after-sales service. We don't patch your existing process. We give you a system with flow - where complaint booking feeds dispatch, dispatch feeds job tracking, tracking feeds parts, parts feed billing, and billing feeds AMC renewal.

Every step automated. Every stage accountable. Every outcome measurable.

System Diagnosis

Six failure modes in manual service operations.

Every unstructured service backend bleeds money, time, and trust.

Manual Assignment

Coordinators manually matching jobs causes dispatch delays and zero accountability.

Zero Visibility

No live view of job stages or technician locations. Issues surface only after escalations.

High Turnaround

Delayed dispatch and unclear job stages lead to long customer wait times and churn.

Revenue Leakage

AMC/EWS renewals slip through. No automated nudges or pipeline tracking.

Spare Parts Loss

Parts untracked and misattributed. No digital trail means no audit or control.

Single-Point Risk

Operations rely on 1-2 people. No redundancy or path to scaling.

Execution Workflow

Two-phase job execution.

Most platforms treat on-site service as a single black box. Fixolutions splits it into distinct, accountable stages.

01

Phase 1: Diagnose

The technician arrives and logs a structured diagnosis before any fix begins.

  • GPS-verified arrival timestamp
  • Root cause identification
  • Spare parts requirement flagging
02

Phase 2: Fix

Only after diagnosis is validated does the technician proceed to resolve.

  • Repair executed against records
  • Parts usage verified & audited
  • Digital report auto-generated
